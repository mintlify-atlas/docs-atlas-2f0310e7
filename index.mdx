<div className="mt-16 mb-16 max-w-5xl mx-auto px-6">
  <h2 className="text-3xl font-bold dark:text-white text-gray-900 mb-4">Quick Start</h2>
  <p className="text-base dark:text-gray-400 text-gray-600 mb-8">Get up and running with SquirrelDisk in minutes</p>
  
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Download SquirrelDisk">
      Visit the [releases page](https://github.com/adileo/squirreldisk/releases) and download the installer for your operating system:
      - **Windows**: `.exe` installer
      - **macOS**: `.dmg` disk image
      - **Linux**: `.deb` package (Ubuntu/Debian)
      
      <Note>The binaries are not code-signed. You may need to bypass security warnings on first launch.</Note>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Install and Launch">
      Run the installer and open SquirrelDisk. On **macOS**, right-click and select **Open** twice to bypass Gatekeeper. On **Windows**, click "More Information" then "Run Anyway" if Windows Defender appears.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Scan Your Disk">
      Select a disk or folder from the main screen and click **Scan**. SquirrelDisk uses parallel scanning powered by Rust to analyze your disk in seconds.
      
      Watch the progress bar as SquirrelDisk traverses your file system and builds a complete map of disk usage.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Explore and Clean Up">
      Navigate the interactive sunburst chart to explore what's taking up space. Drag files and folders to the deletion area to clean up your disk — it's that simple.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
